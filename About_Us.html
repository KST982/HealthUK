About Us!

We at HealthUK are passionate with using the latest technology to create products that make a difference to everyday lives. This is why we are developing and prototyping the world's first universal bio-metric scanner that can measure heart rate, skin temperature, respiratory rates and (for the first time) blood sugar levels.

We are also developing a small mechanism that (inconjunction with HealthUK bio-metric scanner) can auto-inject the user with insulin when there reported blood sugar level is low, not only is this a safe proceedure, it is also completely painless (you wouldn't feel a thing!).
